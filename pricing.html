Payment Structure

As a general rule, 50% of the payment needs to made at the beginning, and 50% just before the delivery of the final material.
In case of research paper representation from our side (i.e. if your submitted abstract is accepted), you need to pay
only ₹ 500 in advance and ₹ 1000 once the paper is accepted by the desired conference/journal and you wish to get it published.
If your abstract is rejected but you still want representation of the paper from our end, an advance of ₹ 1000 needs to paid
and another ₹ 1000 once your paper is accepted by the desired conference/journal and you wish to get it published. In addition to this, you will be charged as per the pricing policy for editing and formatting with a 50% discount on the total amount.

For e.g. -
Your paper of 8 pages (Approx 3000 words) having expected Impact Factor 0.8 was rejected by us but you want us to represent it after necesaary editing.
The conference expects you to format it in MS Word.
Editing charges = 50 x (3000/100) = ₹ 1500
Formatting charges = 150 x 8 = ₹ 1200
Total = ₹ 2700
Discount offered = 50% i.e. ₹ 1350
Total Advance Amount to be Paid = 1000 + 675 = ₹ 1675
Balance amount = 1000 + 675 = ₹ 1675 which needs to be paid once the paper is accepted at the desired conference/journal and you wish to go ahead with its publication.
