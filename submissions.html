Submission Procedure

Please mail the following -

  1. Title
  2. Abstract (or rough idea in less than 200 words)
  3. Name of authors (Not more than 3)
  4. Current word and image count
  5. Your short bio and contact details

along with a .doc, .docx or .txt copy of your latest revision of the material to

ojha.iiitm@gmail.com

Please avoid sending any PDF files.
